## Pricing Conversation Starters

When prospects ask about rates:

### **For Hourly Work**
"I charge $150-250/hour depending on the engagement. Quick reviews and consultations start at $150/hr. Complex implementation work is $200-250/hr. Volume discounts available for longer engagements (10% off at 100+ hours)."

### **For Project Work**
"For project work, I typically charge $X based on scope. Let me understand your requirements and I can give you a fixed quote with timeline."

### **For Retainers**
"Many companies benefit from ongoing support. Retainers typically start at $3,000/month for part-time support up to $15,000/month for more substantial commitment. Includes priority support and strategic guidance."

### **For Full-Time**
"I can take on full-time contracts for major transformation initiatives. Rates are negotiable based on scope and duration."
